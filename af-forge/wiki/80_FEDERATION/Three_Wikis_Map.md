# Three Wikis Map

This page is the federation gateway from the AF-FORGE side.

AF-FORGE should not feel like an isolated machine notebook.
It is one third of a larger system:
- **AF-FORGE** for infrastructure and operational reality
- **arifOS** for governance and runtime law
- **GEOX** for Earth-domain truth

## One stack, three organs

| Wiki | Role | Main question |
|------|------|---------------|
| **AF-FORGE** | machine / operations | what is running, what is exposed, what broke, how do we recover it? |
| **arifOS** | constitutional runtime | what is allowed, what are the floors, what verdict governs action? |
| **GEOX** | Earth witness | what is physically true in geoscience and subsurface reasoning? |

## Navigation rule

### Stay in AF-FORGE when
- the issue is VPS health
- the issue is containers, ports, domains, logs, deploys, compose, or recovery
- the machine state itself is the problem

### Jump to arifOS when
- the issue is authority
- the issue is execution boundaries
- the issue is floor logic, holds, verdicts, or agent/runtime governance

Suggested arifOS entry points:
- `/root/arifOS/wiki/index.md`
- `Federation_Three_Wikis`
- `Naming_Canon`
- `What-is-arifOS`
- `Floors`
- `Concept_Deployment_Architecture`

### Jump to GEOX when
- the issue is geological or Earth-domain truth
- the issue is petrophysics, basins, materials, seismic, or uncertainty in subsurface claims

Suggested GEOX entry points:
- `/root/GEOX/wiki/index.md`
- `00_INDEX/Three_Wikis_Gateway.md`
- `10_THEORY/Theory_of_Anomalous_Contrast.md`
- `20_PHYSICS/EARTH_CANON_9.md`

## Preferred order for cross-system work

Use this stack when all three matter:
1. **AF-FORGE** for machine reality
2. **arifOS** for governance and permission
3. **GEOX** for domain truth

That order keeps the system honest:
- no domain claim without a running substrate when runtime matters
- no machine action without governance
- no elegant explanation without grounded Earth truth
